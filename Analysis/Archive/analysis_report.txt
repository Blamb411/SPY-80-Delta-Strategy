BACKGROUND: SEEKING ALPHA SERVICES
================================================================================

Alpha Picks
-----------
- Launched mid-2022; stock recommendation service based on SA's Quant Ratings
- 2 picks per month (1st and 15th)
- Selection criteria: must maintain "Strong Buy" Quant Rating for 75+ consecutive
  days, US common stocks, market cap >$500M, share price >$10, no REITs
- Quant system factors: Value, Growth, Profitability, Momentum, EPS Revisions
  across ~5,000 stocks
- Style: buy-and-hold; exit signals sent when ratings change
- Founded by Steven Cress
- SA-reported performance: +284% total return vs +83% SPY since launch (Dec 2025)
- Pricing: $449/year

PRO Quant Portfolio (PQP)
-------------------------
- Launched June 2025; much newer than Alpha Picks
- Top 30 stocks scored across Value, Growth, Profitability, Momentum, EPS Revisions
- GARP-plus strategy; includes ADRs and all market caps (no restrictions)
- Equal-weight, fixed-capital approach
- Rebalances every Monday at NYSE open using Friday closing prices
- ~2-3 trades per week; stocks rated "Hold" are removed each Monday
- SA-reported performance: +38.93% in first 6 months vs +6.72% for S&P 500 EWI
  (as of Nov 2025)
- Available only to Seeking Alpha PRO subscribers
- Model portfolio only (not a real brokerage account)

Key Difference: Alpha Picks is passive (accumulate 2 stocks/month, hold).
PQP is active (30-stock portfolio rebalanced weekly with frequent rotation).

================================================================================
ANALYSIS METHODOLOGY
================================================================================

Data Collection:
- Excel spreadsheet with two tabs: ProQuant (1,108 rows of weekly rebalancing
  actions) and AlphaPicks (97 picks from Jul 2022 to Jan 2026)
- Fetched daily OHLCV prices for all 155 unique symbols + SPY from Massive API
- Prices cached in local SQLite database (price_cache.db)

Alpha Picks Portfolio Simulation:
- Equal dollar investment ($1,000) per pick on pick date
- Shares = $1,000 / buy_price at entry
- Position held until close date (if closed) or end of analysis period
- Portfolio value = sum of all active positions valued at daily close
- Daily return = change in total portfolio value
- 97 picks total, max 49 concurrent positions

ProQuant Portfolio Simulation:
- Weight-based daily returns: on each rebalance date, record portfolio weights
  from Excel; between rebalances, compute daily portfolio return as weighted
  average of individual stock daily returns
- Equal-weight ~30 stocks, rebalanced weekly
- Starting value $10,000

Risk Metrics:
- Sharpe Ratio: annualized (mean/std * sqrt(252)) from daily returns
- Sortino Ratio: annualized using downside deviation only
- Calmar Ratio: annualized return / max drawdown
- Probabilistic Sharpe Ratio (PSR): Bailey & Lopez de Prado (2012), probability
  that true Sharpe exceeds benchmark, adjusting for skewness and kurtosis
- Minimum Track Record Length (minTRL): observations needed for 95% confidence
  that Sharpe > 0

KEY FINDINGS
================================================================================

1. ALPHA PICKS HAS A STATISTICALLY SIGNIFICANT EDGE
   - Sharpe of 2.14 with PSR(SR>1) = 99% over 897 days is strong
   - Well exceeds minimum track record of 128 days
   - Positive skewness (1.54) means the upside tail is larger than downside
   - However, excess kurtosis of 14.15 means extreme moves in both directions

2. ALPHA PICKS EDGE IS FADING
   - Annual Sharpe: 3.92 (2022) -> 3.43 (2023) -> 1.51 (2024) -> 0.50 (2025)
   - Max drawdown growing: 12.5% -> 12.3% -> 22.1% -> 38.5%
   - 2025 PSR(SR>0) only 69% -- barely suggestive of positive performance
   - Possible causes: strategy crowding as service grew, regime shift, mean reversion

3. PROQUANT TOO NEW FOR DEFINITIVE CONCLUSIONS
   - 167 days, 4 short of the 171-day minimum track record
   - PSR(SR>0) = 94.8% is promising but not yet at 95% threshold
   - Beat SPY in return (+40% vs +17%) but with 2.5x the volatility
   - SPY actually had a BETTER Sharpe (2.32 vs 2.03) during this period

4. BOTH STRATEGIES CARRY SIGNIFICANT RISK
   - Alpha Picks volatility (44%) is 2.7x SPY
   - ProQuant volatility (27%) is 2.5x SPY
   - Alpha Picks max drawdown of 38.5% (early 2025) is double SPY's 19%
   - Higher returns are compensation for higher risk, not purely "alpha"

5. PORTFOLIO CONSTRUCTION MATTERS
   - Total invested: $97,000 across 97 picks
   - Final value: $103,846 -- only $6,846 in absolute profit
   - The +1,980% "total return" reflects portfolio value growth from the
     initial positions, heavily driven by early picks (APP, CLS, POWL, STRL)
   - Later picks contribute less because early winners dominate portfolio weight
   - This is a known issue with buy-and-hold equal-dollar portfolios

ALPHA PICKS — ENTRY TIMING VERIFICATION
-----------------------------------------
Alpha Picks are disclosed at noon on the 1st and 15th of each month.
The SA portfolio records the purchase at the "pick day" close price.

  Buy price matches:
    pick_day_close:   43% of picks
    next_day_close:   22%
    other:            29%
    prev_day_close:    3%
    next_day_open:     2%

  Conclusion: SA uses the pick-day close price ~43% of the time, but
  the match is inconsistent. Real investors buying after the noon
  announcement would get approximately the pick-day close price.

-----------------------------------
  The announcement itself moves the stock. Measuring from T-1 close:

  T+1 to T+252        65  +33.97%    +19.75%  +14.22%    71%     57%

  CRITICAL: The announcement day pop is GROWING year over year:
    2022: +0.53% avg announcement pop (8 picks)

    2024: +2.43% avg announcement pop (24 picks)
    2025: +4.13% avg announcement pop (24 picks)

  This is the smoking gun for Sharpe decay: as subscriber count grows,

EXIT STRATEGY BACKTESTER RESULTS
================================================================================

Script: alpha_picks_backtester.py

Tested 21 strategy variants on Alpha Picks. All use $1,000 per pick,
same picks and entry dates. Only exit rules change.

TOP STRATEGIES (ranked by Sharpe):

  #  Strategy                    Sharpe    PSR   Return   MaxDD   Ann.Ret
  1  Vol-Adjusted Sizing          2.60   100%  +2149%   31.6%   +168%
  2  Vol + SL25% + Trail30%       2.46   100%  +1610%   24.6%   +143%
  3  Vol + SL20% + Trail25%       2.43   100%  +1420%   21.1%   +134%
  4  BASELINE (SA exits only)     2.29   100%  +1980%   38.5%   +158%
  5  SL25% + Trail30%             2.19   100%  +1544%   30.9%   +138%

KEY FINDINGS FROM EXIT STRATEGIES:
  - Vol-adjusted sizing is the clear winner: +0.31 Sharpe over baseline
    with LESS drawdown (31.6% vs 38.5%)
  - Stop losses HURT total returns (cut momentum winners too early)
  - Trailing stops also hurt: they exit during normal pullbacks in
    multi-bagger stocks like APP (+1,300%), CLS (+700%), POWL (+400%)
  - Max holding periods reduce returns without improving Sharpe
  - BEST COMBO: vol-adjusted sizing alone (inverse vol weighting,
    target 1% daily risk per position, capped at 3x base)
